Homexperia

An AI-powered home discovery platform — helping people find and explore properties through a smarter, more personal search experience.

Visit Live Site
ClientHomexperia
ServicesUX/UI · AI Web App
Year2025
RoleSenior UI/UX Designer
Overview

Homexperia technologies offers a technology-driven platform that utilizes AR/VR, AI/ML to solve visualization challenges in the home décor industry. We help manufacturers, brands, designers, and architects showcase their products effectively and provide customers with an enhanced shopping experience.

The Challenge

Home decor shopping often makes it difficult for customers to visualize how products will look in their own spaces, leading to uncertainty and delayed purchasing decisions. At the same time, manufacturers, brands, designers, and architects face challenges in showcasing their products in an engaging and realistic way across digital channels.

The Approach

We designed an AI-powered platform that leverages AR/VR and machine learning to deliver immersive, interactive visualization experiences. By combining intuitive user journeys with realistic product previews and personalized recommendations, we created a seamless solution that helps businesses showcase their offerings effectively while giving customers the confidence to make informed design and purchasing decisions.

Process

From insight to a refined experience

01

Discovery Workshop & Research

Aligned on goals and studied how people search for homes — uncovering needs, behaviours, and pain points.

02

Visual Exploration

Explored moodboards, directions, and references to define a look and feel that feels trustworthy and modern.

03

Wireframing

Mapped layouts and flows for search, results, and property detail to validate structure before visuals.

04

Visual Designs

Crafted clean, high-fidelity screens with a clear hierarchy that guides users from intent to enquiry.

05

Prototyping

Built interactive prototypes to test key journeys and refine interactions, motion, and edge cases.

06

Design Systems

Delivered a reusable component library and tokens for consistency and a smooth developer handoff.
