About Me

Designing with empathy, delivering with precision

Hi, I'm Ravi Patel, a Freelance UI/UX Designer with 7+ years of experience creating intuitive, user-centered digital products. I hold a Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) degree from IIIT Vadodara, where I built a strong foundation in technology and problem-solving that complements my design expertise.

As a freelance designer, I've partnered with startups, enterprises, and growing businesses to craft seamless web and mobile experiences across industries such as healthcare, AI, IoT, education, and enterprise software. My work focuses on user research, wireframing, prototyping, design systems, and high-fidelity interfaces that balance aesthetics with functionality.

I'm passionate about transforming complex challenges into simple, meaningful, and engaging experiences. Whether designing an MVP, improving an existing product, or building a scalable design system, I strive to create solutions that deliver real value to both users and businesses.

I enjoy collaborating with innovative teams, learning new technologies, and helping turn ideas into impactful digital products.
